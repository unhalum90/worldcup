[ONE THING TO KEEP IN MIND]

"Here's what you need to know about Guadalajara:

This is tequila country, and the hospitality is incredible - but the altitude and heat can sneak up on you.

Guadalajara sits at about 5,100 feet elevation. Not as extreme as Mexico City, but if you're coming from sea level, take it easy the first day. Add in June temperatures in the mid-80s Fahrenheit, and dehydration is real.

Drink plenty of water - especially if you're also enjoying the local tequila. Pace yourself, and you'll have an amazing time."
